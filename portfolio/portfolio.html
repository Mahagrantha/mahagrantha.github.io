How I work

a repeatable process
01

Understand the system

Use the product myself, sit in on engineering standups, and interview the subject-matter experts who already know the gaps in the docs.

02

Map the audience and the job

Define who is reading, what they already know, and the single task they need to complete — before deciding on format or tone.

03

Outline before drafting

Structure first: headings, steps, and a consistent template, so the writing fills in a shape rather than inventing one as it goes.

04

Test, ship, and maintain

Run a usability pass and an SME review, publish, then track where readers drop off and feed that back into the next revision.
