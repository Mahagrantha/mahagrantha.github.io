Roles & access

What each role can see and do on the platform.

RoleTypical access
AdministratorFull access — products, orders, inventory, marketplaces, finance, reports, and user management.
Sales ExecutiveProducts and orders. Can create and update listings, view order status.
Operations ExecutiveOrders, inventory, and marketplaces. Owns the fulfillment workflow and sync recovery.
Finance ExecutiveInvoices, settlements, and financial reports. Read access to orders for reconciliation.
Warehouse ExecutiveInventory only — stock levels, adjustments, and reorder thresholds.

API keys inherit the permissions of the role they were generated under, so a Warehouse Executive's key can only call inventory endpoints even if the request is otherwise valid.

PART 2 · KNOWLEDGE BASE

Frequently asked questions

Why can't I move an order straight from "new" to "shipped"?

Orders must follow the standard fulfillment sequence: new → payment confirmed → processing → packed → shipped → delivered. Skipping a step returns an invalid_status_transition error. This keeps the audit trail accurate for every order.

A product won't save — it says the SKU already exists. What do I do?

SKUs must be unique across the whole catalog. Search the catalog for the SKU first — it may belong to a deactivated product. If so, reactivate that product instead of creating a new one.

How do I know if a product is low on stock?

Each product has a status of in_stock, low_stock, or out_of_stock, based on its current quantity against its reorder threshold. Low-stock items also trigger an inventory.low_stock alert if webhooks are configured.

Why did a marketplace sync fail?

Most often it's an expired OAuth connection or a temporary issue on the marketplace's side. Try a manual sync first; if it keeps failing, the channel likely needs to be reconnected from the dashboard. See the Marketplace Sync Recovery SOP.

Where do I see GST on an order?

The invoice for each order breaks out the subtotal, GST rate, GST amount, and final total. Pull it from the order's invoice record or generate a tax report for a date range.

Can a Sales Executive deactivate a user?

No — user management is restricted to Administrators. A Sales Executive's key will receive a 403 Forbidden if it attempts to call a Users endpoint.

Glossary

SKU
Stock Keeping Unit — the unique code identifying a specific product or variant in the catalog.
Channel
A connected marketplace (e.g. Amazon, Flipkart, Shopify) that orders import from and listings sync to.
Reorder threshold
The stock quantity at which a product is flagged low_stock and an alert is triggered.
Settlement
A payout record from a marketplace, itemizing fees and commissions deducted from order revenue.
Sync
The process of pushing product and stock data from La Poche to a connected marketplace, or pulling orders in.
Fulfillment workflow
The fixed sequence an order moves through: new → payment confirmed → processing → packed → shipped → delivered.
Webhook
An automatic notification sent to a configured endpoint when an event occurs (e.g. order.created), instead of polling the API.
