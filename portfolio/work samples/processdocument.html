PROCESS DOCUMENTATION

Operations manual

Step-by-step internal workflows for the teams running day-to-day operations on La Poche — who owns each step, what triggers it, and how long it should take.

Each process below states its trigger, the owning role for every step, expected turnaround, and what to do when something falls outside the normal path. Pair this with the User Guide for where to click, and the API reference for automating any of these steps.

CHAPTER 01

Purpose & scope

Purpose

This manual defines the standard operating procedures for teams using La Poche day to day. Where the User Guide explains what a screen does, this document explains what should happen, in what order, and who is responsible — so the same order, return, or product launch is handled the same way regardless of who's on shift.

Scope

Covers order fulfillment, product and marketplace onboarding, returns, inventory and settlement reconciliation, user lifecycle management, and escalation paths. It does not cover marketing, supplier negotiation, or warehouse layout — those sit outside La Poche.

HOW TO READ THIS DOCUMENT

Each process is shown as a sequence of steps. Every step names the role responsible and the expected turnaround time, so handoffs between teams are unambiguous.

CHAPTER 03

Order fulfillment process

Trigger: New order imported from any connected marketplaceTarget turnaround: Delivered within 5 business days of payment confirmation
Confirm payment
Sales Executive · within 2 hours

Verify the order's payment status matches what the marketplace reports, then move it to Payment Confirmed.

Generate pick list
Operations Executive · same business day

Move the order to Processing and pull items against the pick list.

Pack and label
Operations Executive · same business day

Mark the order Packed once items are boxed; this triggers the courier label for integrated shipping methods.

Hand off to courier
Operations Executive · within 1 business day of packing

Enter the tracking number and mark the order Shipped.

Issue invoice
Finance Executive · same day as shipping

Confirm billing and GST details are complete, then generate the invoice from the order page.

Confirm delivery
Sales Executive · on courier confirmation

Mark the order Delivered once the courier confirms — automatically where integrated, manually otherwise.

EXCEPTION

If an order remains on New Order for more than 4 hours, the Sales Executive escalates to the source marketplace directly rather than waiting indefinitely for payment confirmation.

CHAPTER 04

Product onboarding process

Trigger: New product approved for saleTarget turnaround: Live on all channels within 1 business day
Assign SKU and category
Warehouse Executive · on intake

Follow the existing SKU pattern for the category; confirm the SKU isn't already in use.

Enter product record
Operations Executive · same day

Add name, variants, price, starting stock, and at least one product image. Leave Status as Inactive until review is complete.

Review for accuracy
Administrator · within 1 business day

Check pricing, category, and images against the source listing or supplier sheet.

Activate and sync
Administrator · on approval

Set Status to Active. La Poche queues the product for sync to every connected marketplace.

Confirm live listing
Operations Executive · within 1 business day of activation

Spot-check the product on at least one connected marketplace to confirm the listing matches.

CHAPTER 05

Marketplace onboarding process

Trigger: Decision to sell on a new channelTarget turnaround: Varies with catalog size
Set up channel account
Administrator · before connecting

Confirm a seller account exists on the target marketplace and required documentation (GSTIN, bank details) is on file with them.

Connect and authorize
Administrator · same day

Connect the channel under Marketplace Integration and authorize with the requested credentials or API key.

Run initial catalog sync
Administrator · sync time depends on catalog size

Sync the full catalog, or select an initial product subset for a phased rollout.

Validate listings
Operations Executive · within 2 business days of sync completing

Spot-check a sample of listings on the new channel for pricing, images, and category mapping.

Brief the team
Administrator · before go-live

Confirm Sales and Operations are aware of any channel-specific quirks — packaging requirements, SLA differences, or return policies.

EXCEPTION

Disconnecting a channel never cancels orders already placed there. Operations must clear all open orders on that channel before an Administrator disconnects it.

CHAPTER 06

Returns & refunds process

Trigger: Customer-initiated return or marketplace return notificationTarget turnaround: Refund issued within 3 business days of receipt
Log the return
Sales Executive · on notification

Record the return against the original order, including the stated reason.

Receive and inspect
Warehouse Executive · on arrival

Inspect the returned item's condition and confirm it matches what was logged.

Restock or write off
Warehouse Executive · same day as inspection

Process the return through Sales Management so stock updates automatically; items unfit for resale are written off, not restocked.

Issue refund
Finance Executive · within 3 business days of receipt

Process the refund and confirm it's reflected against the original payment method.

CHAPTER 07

Inventory reconciliation process

Trigger: Scheduled — weekly, plus any low-stock alertTarget turnaround: Discrepancies resolved within 1 business day
Run physical count
Warehouse Executive · weekly, scheduled

Count physical stock for the week's assigned category and compare against La Poche's recorded quantity.

Log discrepancies
Warehouse Executive · same day

Apply a manual stock adjustment for any mismatch, with a reason — recount, damage, or unrecorded return.

Investigate recurring mismatches
Administrator · within 1 business day

If the same SKU mismatches repeatedly, check for stock edits made directly on a marketplace outside La Poche.

Respond to low-stock alerts
Warehouse Executive · within 1 business day of alert

Confirm whether to reorder, and update the reorder threshold if it no longer reflects lead time.

CHAPTER 08

Settlement reconciliation process

Trigger: Marketplace settlement receivedTarget turnaround: Reconciled within 5 business days
Pull settlement report
Finance Executive · on receipt

Generate the Settlement Report for the period and channel in question.

Match orders to payout
Finance Executive · within 3 business days

Confirm each order in the settlement matches a recorded order and that commission and fee deductions are itemized correctly.

Flag mismatches
Finance Executive · same day as found

Escalate any payout that doesn't match the expected total to the marketplace's seller support.

Close the period
Administrator · within 5 business days

Sign off once all line items are matched or formally flagged as disputed.

CHAPTER 09

User on/offboarding process

Trigger: New hire start date, or employee departureTarget turnaround: Access granted/revoked same day

Onboarding

Determine role
Administrator · before start date

Choose the most restrictive role that still covers the person's responsibilities — see Chapter 2.

Create account
Administrator · on start date

Add the user with their work email; they receive an invitation to set a password.

Confirm access
Administrator · within 1 business day

Verify the new user can see the modules their role requires and nothing beyond it.

Offboarding

Deactivate account
Administrator · same day as departure

Deactivate the user. Any API keys generated under their account are revoked immediately.

Reassign ownership
Administrator · within 1 business day

Transfer any in-progress orders, open returns, or scheduled reports to another team member.
